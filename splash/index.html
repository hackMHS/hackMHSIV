MillburnSPLASH

Millburn Splash is an afternoon of mini, interactive classes for students in grades 1-8, taught by students at Millburn High School. The classes cover a wide variety of topics, from origami to entrepreneurship to robotics!, For this splash we are taking the help of fellow parents to make sure splash is well organized.

All proceeds from Millburn Splash support hackMHS. hackMHS is a student-organized event encouraging high school students to learn to code apps, games, and websites. To learn more about hackMHS, click here.

frequently asked questions

  • Who can register for classes?

    Our classes are designed for students in grades 1-8. Students in grades 9-12 should consider attending hackMHS for code and technology-related workshops!

  • Can parents accompany their children?

    Yes! Parents are encouraged to attend. There is no additional cost for parents attending, but due to classroom limitations, each student may only be accompanied by one parent.

  • How much does it cost to attend?

    Splash 2018 has a new registration system and prices can be viewed on our prices page below.

  • When is It?

    There are multiple splash dates, and can be viewed below.

  • Who will be teaching the classes?

    Students at Millburn High School involved in organizing hackMHS will be teaching classes at Millburn Splash.

  • How do I get there?

    Millburn Splash takes place at 462 Millburn Avenue, Millburn, NJ. For Google Maps directions, click here.

  • I have a question not answered here.

    Feel free to email us at hackatmhs@gmail.com, and we'd be happy to answer your question!
